That Time I Overreacted and Bought a Macbook

That summer break, my roommate always told me that Macbook is incredible and I should give it a shot. I felt a little crazy because it would cost me one thousand dollars, which is a bunch of money for me. I hesitated for several days.

In the end, I made a difficult decision. I emptied my pocket and bought a Macbook.
